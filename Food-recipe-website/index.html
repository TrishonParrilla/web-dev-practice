SGT Garci

  • Prep: 15 minutes
  • Rise: 1-2 hours
  • Bake: 10-12 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • Dough (1 large pizza or 2 medium)
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (about 110°F)
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour (or bread flour for chewier crust)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Sauce
  • ½ cup crushed tomatoes or pizza sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • Toppings (choose your adventure)
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella
  • Pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers
  • Fresh basil (optional)Red pepper flakes (optional)
